# PadhoGuru-CrossPlatform 📒🚀

**PadhoGuru** is a comprehensive academic productivity suite built with **Flutter**. It is designed to help students manage their university life seamlessly, featuring an automated timetable parser, task management, and a dedicated self-study dashboard.

This repository represents the evolved, cross-platform version of the original Kotlin-based PadhoGuru.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📅 Smart Timetable Management
*   **Excel Auto-Identification:** Upload your university timetable in `.xlsx` format. The app automatically identifies subjects, timings, and rooms.
*   **Class Detail View:** Tap any class to see extended details, including the **Subject Teacher** and **Room Number**.
*   **Clear & Sync:** Quickly reset your schedule to upload new revisions without data overlap.

### ⚡ Performance Dashboard (Self-Study)
*   **Dynamic Stat Cards:** Track your total study sessions and high-priority goals at a glance.
*   **Live Session Tracking:** A "LIVE" indicator appears automatically when your current time matches a scheduled study slot.
*   **Category Tagging:** Organize sessions into *Study*, *Practice*, or *Crucial* categories.

### 📝 Productivity Tools
*   **Sticky Notes:** A grid-based "Quick Notes" section with a retro-vintage aesthetic for rapid thought-catching.
*   **Task Manager:** Track assignments and deadlines with a dedicated status-toggle system.

### 🎨 Premium UI/UX
*   **Adaptive Theme Engine:** Seamless switching between Light and Dark modes.
*   **Indigo Aesthetic:** A clean, professional color palette designed for high focus and low eye strain.
*   **Intuitive Navigation:** Side drawer and bottom navigation for rapid access to all modules.
